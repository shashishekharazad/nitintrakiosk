NITJ Intra Kiosk

Welcome to NITJ Intra Kiosk, a digital initiative meticulously crafted by Shashi Shekhar Azad and Saurav Kumar, dedicated computer science students at the esteemed National Institute of Technology Jalandhar. Motivated by a shared passion for enhancing the academic experience, we embarked on this journey to create a comprehensive platform tailored for our fellow engineering enthusiasts.

At NITJ Intra Kiosk, we strive to empower the vibrant community of NIT Jalandhar by offering a diverse range of services. As students ourselves, we understand the importance of readily accessible resources. Our website serves as a one-stop destination for study materials, notes, books, previous year question papers, and a wealth of campus information. Whether you're navigating the intricacies of coursework or seeking insights into the diverse array of societies and clubs on campus, NITJ Intra Kiosk is designed to cater to your every need.

Driven by a commitment to excellence, Shashi and Saurav have harnessed their technical prowess to create a user-friendly and dynamic platform. As we continue to evolve, our mission remains rooted in providing invaluable support to the engineering community at NIT Jalandhar. Join us in this collaborative journey where knowledge meets innovation, and together, let's elevate the academic experience at NITJ through NITJ Intra Kiosk.

Shashi Shekhar Azad
