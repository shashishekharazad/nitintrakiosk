Welcome to NITJ Intra Kiosk

We're here to make your engineering journey smoother by providing easy access to top-notch study materials. At NITJ Intra Kiosk, we understand the challenges you face in your academic pursuits. Our platform offers a range of study resources specifically tailored for NIT Jalandhar students. Whether you're tackling undergraduate or postgraduate studies, our curated collection of materials is designed to support you.

We're more than just a study material repository; we're a community-driven initiative that encourages collaboration and knowledge sharing. Explore our user-friendly interface to find comprehensive lecture notes, reference materials, practice exams, and research resources across various engineering disciplines. Stay ahead in your studies with the latest updates and supplemental materials aligned with NIT Jalandhar's curriculum. NITJ Intra Kiosk is not just a website; it's a portal to a community where education meets innovation.
Join us on this journey of academic excellence.
